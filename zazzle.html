    MOMENTO BY ZAZZLE

    Designing for relationships, memories and nostalgia.












    What is Momento

    Momento is a conceptual service by Zazzle that taps into the power of relationships. Momento allows people to appreciate their relationships through active reminiscing and personalized gift-giving.

    About Zazzle: Zazzle is an online marketplace for custom products that allows designers and customers to co-create and personalize their products (t-shirts, coffee mugs, stamps, posters etc).




    HOW MIGHT WE

    How might we help Zazzle customers to express their love and strengthen their existing relationships ?

    MOMENTO APP

    Deeper engagement with memories

    A dedicated service to look back at your memories with a loved one puts user in a different context. In a more relationship focused environment than any service currently available to them.

    Active reminiscing of shared moments

    People engage with the memory and remember a lot of details when prompted to reminisce about the photo. The prompt helps the nostalgia set in, which helps in coming up with meaningful and relatable gift ideas.

    Reduced anxiety in personalizing gifts

    By giving context to gifts through moments, people now find the personalization process a lot easier putting more effort into the thought rather than the gift.

    Key INSIGHTS

    People are using Zazzle to personalize products and express Love.

    Team in it’s reserach found that customers at Zazzle were personalizing products out of the following needs:
      - To validate their relationships
      - To show an investment of time and effort
      - To evoke emotions in themselves or others or both

    And most importantly,
    To express Love. Love for yourself and love for others.

    Personalizing a product is not an easy task.

    Talking to Zazzle customers we found that:
      - There was an incredible amount of anxiety in the entire process of making a custom product.
      - People didn’t usually know what they want, which made having a lot of choice on the Zazzle website an issue.
      - Customers had low confidence in their ability to design a “good looking” product.
