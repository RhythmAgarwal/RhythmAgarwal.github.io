Hello!

I am a UX Designer who cares equally about why we should build, what we should build and how we should build it. While the ‘whys’ come naturally to me, I have learnt the ‘whats’ and the ‘hows’ by being in the design field for 7 years. I understand that the most important task I have is to design with responsibility. I know the importance of collaboration and having diverse views while designing. Above all, I believe in continuous learning and therefore value the iterative and lean nature of design.
