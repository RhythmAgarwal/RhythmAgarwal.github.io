InMobi / MIIP

Transforming mobile advertising into moments of discovery for consumers










PROBLEM

InMobi is an ad-serving company. Their business model revolves around serving ads on free mobile apps. InMobi Experience Team believed that these traditional ads if not annoying were unappealing and intrusive.

About MIIP

Unlike traditional push-based advertising, Miip will capture a consumer’s attention through stories stitched together based on a strong unifying theme.

For instance, a user who has shown interest in fashion will see an intelligently crafted story with curated recommendations across relevant merchandise with an ability to buy within one discovery zone.

Process - Design Sprints

This project was highly iterative nature. We ran weekly design sprints which ended with A/B tests of team’s designs.

A team of business analyst, data analyst, UI developers and designers worked collboratively to run tests on Beta accounts.

Prototypes





IMPACT

Rapid prototyping and A/B testing led to increasing the CTRs and engagement through mobile advertisements up to 30%. Company decided to launch the initiative as full fledged product and pivoted its philosophy around the concept of immersive user experience.
