Feedwiser - Mobile and Desktop App

Communication network between the merchants and their customers.


Duration:
2 months
Type:
Freelance Work
Roles and Responsibilities:
Design Strategy, UX Design





Project Brief


With the explosion of communication channels, the average merchant or consumer finds it hard to effectively communicate with each other. The mediums – whether it is a phone call, email, text message, chat messengers and other customer service systems – are hard to use. FeedWiser is a quest to improve the way businesses communicate with the consumers.


Solution


The solution involved a mobile app for consumers and a desktop app for service-based businesses. Consumers could provide their feedback when they buy a service. They would be rewarded by the business in the form of coupons and offers for this. Businesses would be able to track customer satisfaction and also increase customer retention.



Design Process


Product Requirement Discussions - Product's design goals were discussed. Design requirements like the ease of communication in the app, workflow, navigation and interactions were finalised.


Quick Mockups - Balsamiq mockups of the flow were made for quick prototyping.

Visual Design for the mobile app and the merchant portal were then designed.
