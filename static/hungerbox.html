Hungerbox- Food Ordering

Meal Ordering Marketplace for working individuals in Bangalore


Duration:
1 month
Type:
Freelance Work
Roles and Responsibilities:
Design Strategy, UX Design





The Problem


Working individuals in Bangalore who are not provided with a food service at their office face the problem of lack of meal choices to order. In India, typical restaurant food ordering usually provides options that serve multiple people and turns out be a costly affair for individuals who want to order on a daily basis.


Solution


Hungerbox is a food ordering marketplace that specifically caters to working individuals enabling them to order personalised meals from a large number of options.


Design Process


The process followed was user-centered and we analysed the food ordering behavior of a working individual. Product Strategy was defined based on the client's business strategy and the user's priorities and preferences.

Design Challenges

Key challenges were-
• To cater to subscription scenarios and handling payment issues related to them.
• Follow slippy UX and make the goal of the user achieve his goal in as little time as possible.
• Cater to scenarios around group ordering.

Lo-fi Wireframing

Once we were done finalizing the product requirements, I created wireframes using Balsamiq for the product taking into account a simple task flow for exploring, choosing, paying for and tracking the meal. After a few iterations, we finalized on the wireframes. Visual Design for the screens were done for the app considering the Android design guidelines and UI patterns.


My Role


It was an individual project so I was leading all aspects of design. I worked very closely with the founders of Hungerbox during this project. I worked with the developers to explain the design and handover the design assets needed for development.
